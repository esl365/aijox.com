# User Interview Template - UI/UX Baseline Research

**Purpose**: Gather qualitative feedback on current UI/UX to identify pain points and opportunities

**Target**: 20 participants (10 teachers, 10 recruiters)

**Timeline**: 1 week

**Duration**: 30-45 minutes per interview

---

## Interview Structure

### Part 1: Introduction (5 minutes)

**Script**:
> "Thank you for taking the time to speak with me today. We're working on improving the Global Educator Nexus platform and would love to hear about your experience using it. This conversation will take about 30-45 minutes. Your feedback is confidential and will help us make the platform better for everyone. Do you have any questions before we begin?"

**Consent**:
- [ ] Permission to record (audio/video)
- [ ] Permission to take notes
- [ ] Confirm anonymity and confidentiality

---

### Part 2: Background (5 minutes)

**Questions**:

1. How long have you been using Global Educator Nexus?
   - [ ] Less than 1 month
   - [ ] 1-3 months
   - [ ] 3-6 months
   - [ ] 6+ months

2. How often do you use the platform?
   - [ ] Daily
   - [ ] Weekly
   - [ ] Monthly
   - [ ] Rarely

3. What device do you primarily use?
   - [ ] Desktop/Laptop
   - [ ] Mobile phone
   - [ ] Tablet
   - [ ] Mix of devices

4. What's your primary goal when using the platform?
   - (Open-ended)

---

### Part 3: First Impressions (10 minutes)

**Task**: Show homepage

**Questions**:

5. When you first landed on our homepage, what was your immediate reaction?
   - (Open-ended - observe body language)

6. What catches your attention first?
   - (Open-ended)

7. Is it immediately clear what this platform does?
   - [ ] Yes - very clear
   - [ ] Somewhat clear
   - [ ] Confusing
   - Follow-up: Why?

8. Do you feel the platform is designed for someone like you?
   - [ ] Yes
   - [ ] No
   - [ ] Unsure
   - Follow-up: What gives you that impression?

9. On a scale of 1-10, how professional does the platform look?
   - Rating: ___/10
   - Follow-up: What would make it a 10?

---

### Part 4: Navigation & Findability (10 minutes)

**Task**: Ask user to complete a typical task (find a job OR post a job)

**Observe**:
- Time to complete: ___ minutes
- Number of clicks: ___
- Hesitations: ___
- Wrong turns: ___
- Did they succeed: [ ] Yes [ ] No

**Questions**:

10. How easy was it to find what you were looking for?
    - [ ] Very easy
    - [ ] Easy
    - [ ] Difficult
    - [ ] Very difficult

11. Was the navigation structure intuitive?
    - (Open-ended)

12. Did you ever feel lost or unsure where to go next?
    - (Open-ended - specific examples)

13. What features do you use most frequently?
    - (List all mentioned)

14. Are those features easy to access?
    - (Open-ended)

---

### Part 5: Role-Specific Questions

#### For Teachers:

15. Walk me through your typical job search process on our platform.
    - (Observe and document workflow)

16. What frustrates you most about searching for jobs?
    - (Open-ended)

17. How do you decide which jobs to apply for?
    - (Open-ended - what information is critical?)

18. Have you used the video resume feature?
    - [ ] Yes → Follow-up: How was that experience?
    - [ ] No → Follow-up: Why not?

19. What would make you more likely to apply to a job?
    - (Open-ended)

20. Have you ever abandoned an application halfway through?
    - [ ] Yes → Follow-up: Why?
    - [ ] No

#### For Recruiters:

15. Walk me through your process of posting a job.
    - (Observe and document workflow)

16. What's the most time-consuming part of recruiting on our platform?
    - (Open-ended)

17. How do you find qualified candidates?
    - (Open-ended)

18. Have you used our AI matching features?
    - [ ] Yes → Follow-up: How helpful was it?
    - [ ] No → Follow-up: Why not?

19. What information do you wish you had about candidates that you don't currently have?
    - (Open-ended)

20. How do you track applications and candidates?
    - (Open-ended)

---

### Part 6: Visual Design & Trust (5 minutes)

21. How does the visual design make you feel?
    - (Open-ended - emotions)

22. Does the platform feel modern and up-to-date?
    - [ ] Yes
    - [ ] No
    - [ ] Neutral

23. Do you trust this platform with your personal information?
    - [ ] Yes → Why?
    - [ ] No → Why not?

24. Are there any design elements that feel unprofessional or outdated?
    - (Open-ended)

---

### Part 7: Comparison (5 minutes)

25. Have you used other job platforms? Which ones?
    - (List all mentioned)

26. How does Global Educator Nexus compare to those platforms?
    - Better: (What aspects?)
    - Worse: (What aspects?)
    - Similar: (What aspects?)

27. What does [competitor platform] do better than us?
    - (Open-ended - specific features)

28. What do we do better than [competitor platform]?
    - (Open-ended)

---

### Part 8: Wishlist (5 minutes)

29. If you could change ONE thing about the platform, what would it be?
    - (Open-ended - most important pain point)

30. What feature or improvement would make you use the platform more frequently?
    - (Open-ended)

31. On a scale of 0-10, how likely are you to recommend this platform to a colleague?
    - Rating: ___/10 (Net Promoter Score)
    - Follow-up: Why that score?

---

### Part 9: Closing (2 minutes)

32. Is there anything else you'd like to share that we haven't covered?
    - (Open-ended)

33. Can we follow up with you if we have additional questions?
    - [ ] Yes - Contact: _______________
    - [ ] No

**Thank the participant**:
> "Thank you so much for your time and honest feedback. Your insights are incredibly valuable and will directly influence how we improve the platform."
