## Video Profile & AI Analysis

### Why Create a Video Profile?

Your video profile is analyzed by our AI Screener (GPT-4o Vision) to provide:
- Teaching style assessment
- Personality trait identification
- Professional presentation feedback
- Profile completeness score
- Personalized improvement suggestions

### Recording Your Video

**Duration:** 2-3 minutes recommended

**What to Include:**
1. **Introduction (30 seconds)**
   - Name and current location
   - Teaching experience summary
   - Subjects you teach

2. **Teaching Philosophy (60 seconds)**
   - Your approach to education
   - What makes you unique
   - Student engagement strategies

3. **Why International Teaching (30 seconds)**
   - Countries you're interested in
   - Cultural adaptability
   - Career goals

4. **Closing (30 seconds)**
   - Key strengths
   - Availability
   - Thank you

**Video Tips:**
- Use good lighting (face the window or use a lamp)
- Quiet background
- Professional attire
- Maintain eye contact with camera
- Speak clearly and confidently
- Smile naturally

### Uploading Your Video

1. Go to "My Profile" → "Video Profile"
2. Click "Upload Video"
3. Select your video file (MP4, MOV, AVI - max 100MB)
4. Wait for upload to complete
5. Click "Analyze Video" to get AI feedback

### Understanding AI Feedback

After analysis (takes 30-60 seconds), you'll receive:

**Teaching Style Assessment:**
- Student-centered vs. Teacher-centered
- Interactive vs. Lecture-based
- Traditional vs. Progressive

**Personality Traits:**
- Enthusiastic
- Patient
- Organized
- Creative
- Approachable

**Strengths:**
- Clear communication
- Passion for teaching
- Subject expertise
- Cultural awareness

**Areas for Improvement:**
- Provide more specific examples
- Demonstrate classroom management
- Show technological proficiency

**Overall Score:** 0-100

**Completeness Score:**
- Profile Photo: ✓

**Next Steps:**
Actionable recommendations to improve your profile.

## Understanding AI Matching

### How It Works

Our Autonomous Headhunter uses a three-stage matching process:

**Stage 1: Semantic Similarity (60% weight)**
- AI analyzes your full profile text
- Creates a 1536-dimensional embedding
- Compares to job description embedding
- Finds semantic matches (not just keywords)

**Stage 2: Visa Eligibility Check**
- Automatically checks visa requirements
- Validates against 10 country rulebooks
- Filters out ineligible positions

**Stage 3: Preference Matching (40% weight)**
- Location preferences
- Salary expectations
- Start date availability
- Teaching subjects
- Grade levels

### Match Score Breakdown

**90-100%: Excellent Match**
- All requirements met
- Strong experience alignment
- Visa eligible
- Preferences aligned

**75-89%: Good Match**
- Most requirements met
- Relevant experience
- Visa eligible
- Some preference misalignment

**60-74%: Moderate Match**
- Basic requirements met
- Some experience gap
- May need additional qualifications

**Below 60%: Low Match**
- Significant requirement gaps
- Consider alternative positions

### Personalized Outreach

When schools use our AI Headhunter, you may receive personalized emails:
- Generated by Claude 3.5 Sonnet
- Tailored to your specific qualifications
- Explains why you're a match
- Direct application link

**Example:**

Subject: International School Seoul - Your English Teaching Experience Matches Perfectly

## Applying for Jobs

### Standard Application

1. Find a job you're interested in
2. Click "Apply Now"
3. Review your profile
4. Add a cover letter (optional but recommended)
5. Attach additional documents if requested
6. Submit application

### Cover Letter Tips

**Do:**
- Personalize for each position
- Address specific job requirements
- Highlight relevant experience
- Express genuine interest in location
- Mention your visa eligibility

**Don't:**
- Use generic templates
- Repeat your entire resume
- Exceed 300-400 words
- Include salary demands (unless requested)

**Example Cover Letter:**
```
Dear Hiring Manager,

I am excited to apply for the High School English Teacher position
at Seoul International Academy. With 5 years of experience teaching
AP English Literature and Language, I am confident I can contribute
to your school's academic excellence.

My background includes:
- Developing rigorous AP English curriculum
- Achieving 90% AP exam pass rate (2019-2023)
- Mentoring new teachers in best practices
- Integrating technology in literature instruction

I am particularly drawn to SIA's emphasis on critical thinking and
global citizenship. Having taught diverse student populations, I
understand the unique challenges and rewards of international education.

As a U.S. citizen with a Bachelor's degree in English and TEFL
certification, I am fully eligible for South Korea's E-2 visa.
I am available to start in August 2025.

Thank you for considering my application. I look forward to discussing
how I can contribute to your school community.

Sincerely,
Sarah Johnson
```

## Interview Process

### Interview Invitation

When a school invites you for an interview:
1. Receive email notification
2. View interview details in dashboard
3. Choose available time slot
4. Add to calendar

### Interview Types

**Video Interview (Most Common)**
- Platform: Zoom, Google Meet, or Microsoft Teams
- Meeting link provided
- Duration: 30-60 minutes
- Camera and microphone required

**Phone Interview**
- International phone call
- Time zone consideration
- Duration: 20-30 minutes

**In-Person Interview**
- For local candidates
- School location
- Travel reimbursement (varies)

### Preparation Tips

**Research the School:**
- Website and mission
- Curriculum (IB, AP, British, etc.)
- Student demographics
- Recent news/achievements

**Common Questions:**
- Why do you want to teach abroad?
- Tell us about your teaching philosophy
- How do you manage classroom behavior?
- Describe a challenging teaching situation
- Why this specific country/school?
- What's your experience with [specific curriculum]?
- When can you start?

**Questions to Ask:**
- Professional development opportunities
- Contract details (salary, housing, flights)
- Class sizes and teaching load
- Support for new teachers
- Visa sponsorship process
- School culture and community

**Technical Setup:**
- Test your internet connection
- Check camera and microphone
- Find quiet, well-lit space
- Have backup plan (phone number)
- Dress professionally

### After the Interview

1. Send thank-you email within 24 hours
2. Wait for school's decision (typically 3-7 days)
3. Check application status regularly
4. Respond promptly to any requests

### Job Offers

When you receive an offer:
1. Review contract carefully
2. Verify:
   - Salary and currency
   - Contract length
   - Benefits (housing, flights, insurance)
   - Visa sponsorship details
   - Start date
   - Probation period
   - Notice period
3. Ask questions if unclear
4. Negotiate if necessary (politely)
5. Accept or decline within deadline

## AI-Powered Candidate Matching

Our Autonomous Headhunter finds and contacts qualified candidates automatically.

### Running a Match

1. Go to job posting
2. Click "Find Candidates"
3. Set matching parameters:
   - Minimum match score (recommended: 70%)
   - Maximum candidates to return (10-50)

4. Click "Run AI Match"
5. Wait 30-60 seconds for results

### Understanding Match Results

**Match Score Components:**
- **Semantic Similarity (60%)**: How well profile matches job description
- **Requirements Met (20%)**: Education, certifications, experience
- **Preferences Aligned (20%)**: Location, salary, availability

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

**Problem: Video upload fails**
- Solution: Check file size (<100MB), format (MP4, MOV, AVI), and internet connection

- Solution: Complete your profile to 100%, add more experience details, update preferences

- Solution: Improve profile completeness, add relevant keywords, update certifications

**Problem: Email not received**
- Solution: Check spam folder, verify email address in settings, whitelist @aijobx.com

**Problem: Can't find applications**
- Solution: Check filters, look in all status tabs, use search function

- Solution: Re-record video with better lighting/audio, provide more specific examples
